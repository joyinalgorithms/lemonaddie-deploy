About Us

Why LEMONADdiE?
It was July 2023 when we got the idea of putting up a business like this, because why not?

Our unica hija really loves lemonade, so we decided to have a lemonade business plus offer some of her favorite go-to snacks. Btw, her nickname is ADDIE, that’s why we named our family business LEMONADdiE.

We are blessed and proud to say that our God is faithful. He can do all things and make the impossible possible. I resigned from my work to start and focus on our business, and while doing this, we are truly amazed at how God provides for us to finish it. Thankful for the people whom God used in order for us to build this. To my lifetime partner, balang araw 0.00 na tayo sa loan obligation.

I truly believe in: “Malayo pa, Pero Malayo Na”

We can’t wait to see your beautiful and happy faces in our physical store located at the back gate of PRMSU Iba, Zambales.

LEMONADdiE offers you the best drinks and snacks while being entertained with board and word games.
